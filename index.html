jikanwari

A collaborative timetable and assignment information platform built for students at our school.

About

jikanwari is a student-built web project designed to help students organize and share school-related information in one place.

It allows timetable information, homework deadlines, submission reminders, and updates to be shared collaboratively within our school community.

Planned Features

  • Shared timetable management
  • Assignment and homework tracking
  • Submission deadline reminders
  • Collaborative editing by students
  • School-specific information sharing

Built as a non-commercial software project for internal school use. Currently under active development.
